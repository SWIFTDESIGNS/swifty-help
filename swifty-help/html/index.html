Real Estate


You can use the /realestate command to view all available properties for sale or rent. The command will provide a list of available properties and their prices. Or keep an eye on social media.


Once you have found a property you are interested in, use the /buyproperty command followed by the property ID to purchase it. You must have enough money in your bank account to complete the purchase. Every city is not the same.


Yes, some properties may be available for rent instead of purchase. Check the property listing or speak with a real estate agent to see if renting is an option.


Use the /sellproperty command followed by the property ID to sell your property. You will receive the sale price directly to your bank account. Every city is not the same.


Yes, you can own multiple properties at the same time as long as you have enough money to purchase them. While checking that the city allows you too.


Yes, you can give other players access to your property by using the /propertyshare command followed by the player's ID. They will be able to access your property as long as you have given them permission. Contact a staff member if you are not sure or its not working.
